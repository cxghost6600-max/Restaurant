Our Story

Cooking is our language of hospitality

LUMIÈRE was born from a simple idea: that a meal can be a memory. Every plate that leaves our kitchen carries the discipline of classical French technique and the curiosity of modern gastronomy.

2014
Chef Antoine Rousseau opens LUMIÈRE with a single promise — no compromise on ingredients.
2017
Awarded our first Michelin star, becoming the youngest kitchen in the city to receive one.
2021
Opened our private cellar room, housing over 4,000 bottles from six countries.
2026
Recognized among the World's 100 Best Restaurants for the third consecutive year.
